Richardson Home Remodeling

In September 2010, an investor purchased a hud foreclosure property in Richardson. The home was built in 1960 and had been neglected for some time.

For this property, the investor wanted to completely remodel the property. after consulting with the owner, SR's team of experts outfitted the home with a brand new kitchen including hard wood cabinets, granite countertops, and stainless steel appliances. The Bathrooms were also completely redone and included beautiful tile work throughout the shower area and floors as well as all new fixtures and cabinets. The living areas were equipped with a combination of new wood floors and carpet for a luxurious look that is also easy to clean.
