About us

No matter what your needs, big or small, Sanchez Remodeling can assist you in achieving your remodeling and repair goals. Sanchez Remodeling owner and operator has been serving residential and small business owners and their remodeling needs in the greater Dallas area since 2003.

Areas served include Dallas and surrounding areas (Addison, Allen, Arlington, Carrollton, Coppell, Downtown, Farmers Branch, Flower Mound, Frisco, Garland, Grand Prairie, Grapevine, Hebron, Highland Park, Irving, Lake Highlands, Lakewood, Lewisville, Little Elm, McKinney, Mesquite, Murphy, Park Cities, Parker, Plano, Preston Hollow, Richardson, Rockwall, Rowlett, Sachse, Sunnyvale, The Colony, University Park, Uptown, White Rock Lake, Wylie).
